Welcome to my personal website. 
I am a PhD student in the [Lab for Artificial Intelligence and Translational Theranostics](http://www.nukmed.insel.ch/de/lehre-und-forschung/research/artificial-intelligence-and-translational-theranostics) at the [Center for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine](https://www.caim.unibe.ch/index_eng.html) of the [University of Bern](https://www.unibe.ch/). 
My research interests are in the area of high-resolution, preclinical Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scanners based on monolithic crystals and SiPMs, and using Deep Learning to predict the scintillation  interaction position.
